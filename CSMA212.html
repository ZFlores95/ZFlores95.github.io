Media Environments

This course involves researching and presenting original designs and system diagrams for mediated experiences in physical spaces. It requires understanding and defining the context and audience for these mediated experiences, as well as the parameters of the audience's interaction in a given space. I learned to create real-time generated graphics and interactive content specifically reacting to the constraints of a given context, audience, or brief. I also apply 2D or 3D media workflows to design assets for use in physical environments and gain an understanding of video signals and projection technology. The course includes building systems to playback pre-authored and generative media, using calibration tools, corner pinning, warping, and blending for successful projection mapping.
